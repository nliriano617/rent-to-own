About Us

The Rent To Own Auto Centers Story

At Rent To Own Auto Centers we know that sometimes bad things can happen to good people, and their credit scores suffer. This is the one reason that we have developed the Rent To Own Auto Centers concept. Now there is an alternative to the high interest, high down-payment car lots!

With our unique and hassle-free program, you can drive a nice, late model vehicle of YOUR choice! No need to worry about your credit score! We do not check credit! We have made it easy to qualify for our program, we don't care if your credit score is 800 or if its 400! We tailor your payment to fit YOUR budget based on the vehicle that YOU choose!

Your payment will include sales tax, license plates, personal property taxes, oil changes, engine, drivetrain and transmission warranty, 24-hour roadside assistance and more! There is never need for a co-signer and you can turn your vehicle in whenever you want for any reason. You will never be "upside-down" in a car loan again!!
