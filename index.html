Our Story

At Rent To Own Auto Centers, we understand that life can throw unexpected challenges, affecting good people's credit scores. This realization led us to develop the Rent To Own Auto Centers concept, providing an alternative to high-interest, high down-payment car lots.

With our innovative and straightforward program, you can choose from a selection of quality, late-model vehicles, regardless of your credit score. We don't perform credit checks, making it easy for you to qualify. Whether your credit score is 800 or 400, we tailor your payment plan to suit your budget and preferences.

Your payment covers everything from sales tax to license plates, personal property taxes, oil changes, and comprehensive warranty coverage for the engine, drivetrain, and transmission. Additionally, you'll enjoy 24-hour roadside assistance and more. With no need for a co-signer, you have the flexibility to return your vehicle whenever you choose, for any reason. Say goodbye to being "upside-down" in a car loan ever again!
