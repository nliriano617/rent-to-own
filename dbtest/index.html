Our Story

At Rent To Own Auto Centers, we understand that life can throw unexpected challenges, affecting good people's credit scores. This realization led us to develop the Rent To Own concept — providing an alternative to high-interest, high down-payment car lots.

With our innovative and straightforward program, you can choose from a selection of quality, late-model vehicles, regardless of your credit score. We don't perform credit checks, making it easy to qualify. Whether your score is 800 or 400, we tailor your payment plan to suit your budget.

With no need for a co-signer, you have the flexibility to return your vehicle whenever you choose, for any reason. Say goodbye to being "upside-down" in a car loan ever again!
